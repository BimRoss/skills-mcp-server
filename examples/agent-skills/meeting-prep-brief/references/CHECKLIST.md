# Meeting prep — extended checklist

Use when the user asks for deeper rigor or repeats this workflow often.

## Discovery

- What changed since last sync?
- What does success look like at **T+24h** after the meeting?

## Stakeholders

- Who can approve trade-offs?
- Who is informed-only?

## Artifacts

- Link or paste agenda doc if any.
- Paste decisions log if recurring series.

## Close

- Confirm explicit next step + owner + date—even if the step is "schedule follow-up."
